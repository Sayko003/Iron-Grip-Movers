Frequently Asked Questions

What does the moving rate include?

Our hourly rate includes everything necessary for a smooth and efficient move. This comprehensive service covers:

  • — Loading and unloading your belongings
  • — Fuel costs and mileage
  • — Standard moving insurance coverage
  • — Use of specialized moving equipment
  • — Furniture disassembly and reassembly
  • — Protective packing for furniture
  • — Taxes and additional fees
  • — Furniture placement at the destination
  • — Navigating stairs and elevators

How do movers ensure the safety of furniture during transportation?

Protecting your furniture is a top priority at Iron Grip. Our professional movers are specifically trained in secure packing methods, carefully using protective padding, blankets, and reliable packing materials. We take every precaution to make sure your furniture arrives safely and intact, free of damage throughout the move.

Does Iron Grip have licensing and insurance?

Yes, Iron Grip is fully licensed and insured. Your items are fully protected by our comprehensive insurance coverage. If necessary, we can provide a general certificate of insurance (COI) or one tailored specifically to your building’s requirements. Please inform us about special insurance documentation needs in advance, allowing 1-3 business days for preparation.

How can I request a moving estimate?

Requesting an accurate moving quote from Iron Grip is simple and convenient. Reach us via email at info@irongripmovers.com or contact us directly through our website. To ensure accuracy, please provide detailed information about your move, including:

  • — Your current and new locations
  • — Approximate size and volume of items
  • — Any special or oversized items requiring special handling
  • — Your preferred moving date and time

Providing comprehensive details will help us deliver a precise and transparent quote tailored specifically for your needs.
